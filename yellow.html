Pay Attention

School is a very important part of your life and can also reward you later in life! So why not pay attention now!!

How to pay Attention

  • Listen-

    • Always be there ready to learn and listen, there is no point in being there if you're not going to listen and pay attention

  • Put phone away-

    • As hard as it may seem just keep your phone in your backpack and really focus on the teacher or assignment. It's a lot easier to focus and get more work done when there is less distractions

  • Get enough sleep-

    • The average teenager needs at least 8-10 hours of sleep EACH night!Sleep is an important factor and make make school a lot easier to pay attention and focus

  • Take notes-

    • Always have a paper and pencil out so you're ready to take notes during class before the class begins
