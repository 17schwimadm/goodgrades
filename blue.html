Study for tests

Don't wait for the last minute to cram for a test, make enough time to study. If you get distracted easily then block the applications that distract you and then unblock it when you are done.

Ways to study

  • Review Material Frequently

    • The first review should come very shortly after the material was first presented and studied.

  • Rewrite notes

    • Don't waste time doing something in class that you can figure out or neaten up at home.Rewrite your notes as soon after the class as possible, while the material is fresh in your mind so that you can fill in any gaps completely from memory.

  • Make flashcards

    • Put the questions on the side you can see when the paper is folded; unfold it to see the answers inside. Keep quizzing yourself until you get all the answers right reliably.

  • Take good notes

    • Learn to take good notes efficiently as your instructors stress important points in class and as you study your assignments. Without notes, you will need to reread and review the entire assignment before a test. With notes, you can recall the main points in just a fraction of the time.
