Turn in Assignments

When teachers give out assignments, make sure you finish them and turn them in for credit. Try your hardest to finish each and every assignment your teacher hands out.

1: Get Organized!

Get a folder for each class to put your assignments in. Folders are very helpful. You do not want to get zeros for losing your assignments in a messy backpack.

2: Listen!

When a teacher is teaching you on how to do the assignment, LISTEN! Don't let other students distract you!

3: Get your assignments done early!

Try and Get every assignment done early. You don't want to be rushing to get your assignment done in class when you could've had it done the other day.
